What is Flash Loan?

Flash Loan is a feature from DeFi that allows someone to quickly borrow Crypto nominal ( Eg. 1,000,000 DAI), refunded in the same hash transaction on the same blockchain, and only cost a little amount of fee.

Flash loans allow a user to borrow as much as they want with zero capital. For instance, if you’d like to borrow $70,000 worth of DIA, a lending protocol instantly gives it to you, but that doesn’t mean it’s yours. You need to do something with the borrowed funds in order to pay back the loan and perhaps pocket the excess amount.

In general, you must have a purpose before borrowing a Flash Loan. For example, you intends to buy ETH, BNB, or any other crypto coin. So if you borrow 1,000,000 DAI, then bought 500 ETH at exchange A and sold back 500 ETH to get DAI at exchange B ( where market A and B has different prices ) and then you get 1,010,000 DAI. Your profit is 10,000 DAI ( exclude the transaction fee ). Note: That the transaction is very fast and recorded in the 1 Blockchain transaction.
